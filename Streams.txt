		2.Streams
		----------

		2.1 What is a stream?
		A stream is a sequence of data that can be processed with operations.

		Think of a stream as a way to operate on data that is flowing from a source (array/arraylist etc) through 
		the stream. 

		Note:
		Intermediate operations on stream always produce another stream and that is how 
		we can chain multiple operations together to manipulate the data as it flows by in stream.
